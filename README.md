# health_companion
Health companion is a health application designed to help users of all background understand their medical status. We have integrated AI into the application that help create visuals for users to better understand their lab results. We have also incorporated AI to generate a clear summary of users lab results so that they can better understand their results and know how to improve or maintain their health. 
Our application incorporate different features that will help every user in managing their health.
## Camera Scanner
### The camera scanner helps users with low vision or vision conditions to scan their prescription/medicine labels if the font is too small.
